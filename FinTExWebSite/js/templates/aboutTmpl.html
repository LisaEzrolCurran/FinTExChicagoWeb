About Us
RAISING THE PROFILE OF FINTECH IN CHICAGO

FinTex Chicago is a non-profit, member-driven industry association serving FinTech Companies in Chicago and providing a single point of access for those looking to do business here.

We help companies of all sizes take advantage of the power of adjacency through the formation of a business cluster focused on FinTech and FinServ in Chicago. This will allow participating companies to improve their businesses by becoming productive and more innovative.

Our objectives are to:  

  • Promote collaboration and business between companies in the region
  • Stimulate innovation and new company creation
  • Retain local talent, especially from universities, and attract others to the region
  • Attract outside investment and business partners

FinTEx Partners with FinTech Sandbox to Promote Growth of the Chicago FinTech Sector

 FinTech Sandbox, a Boston based nonprofit that helps fintech startups access data and build great products, and FinTEx, a Chicago based nonprofit building a regional fintech cluster, today announced a formal partnership. FinTech Sandbox provides free access to financial data and infrastructure for qualified startups. FinTEx will help FinTech Sandbox engage with the most promising Chicago-based fintech startups. In return, FinTEx-affiliated startups will receive preferred consideration when applying to the Sandbox program. FinTEx and FinTech Sandbox will also develop co-marketing, networking and educational opportunities for the Chicago fintech community, and explore ways to better engage Chicago with the greater fintech ecosystem.
