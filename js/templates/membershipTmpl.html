Membership in FinTEx Chicago

Benefits of Membership

  • Connections to the latest industry intelligence and thought leaders
  • Ability to participate in member only programs
  • Access to member only events
  • Member rates for all employees at all FinTEx Chicago events
  • Listing in the FinTEx member directory
  • Opportunities to share your stories and celebrate your achievements:
    • On the FinTEx Chicago web site
    • On tweets from @FinTExChicago


Membership Details

  • Two classes of Memberships:
    • Regular: For FinTech Companies and individuals with FinTech subject matter expertise who are located in Chicago.
      Regular Application
    • Associate: For FinTech Companies and individuals with FinTech subject matter expertise who are not located in Chicago and for non FinTech companies located in Chicago.
      Associate Application
